Cool Sites I Recommend

Welcome to my blogroll! Here you'll find links to friends' personal websites, cool discoveries I've made while surfing the web, and other sites that capture the spirit of the early internet that I love so much.

If you have a personal website with a similar vibe, let me know and maybe we can exchange links!

~ Friends' Personal Sites ~

PixelPusher's Digital Playground - My friend's amazing collection of pixel art and retro game reviews.
Cassette Memories - Julie's nostalgic blog about 80s and 90s music and culture.
8-Bit Dreams - Carlos documents his journey restoring vintage computers.
Digital Archaeologist - Sarah's fascinating exploration of forgotten websites and internet history.
The Blinking Text - Mike's collection of web design tutorials like it's 1999.

~ Retro Web Inspiration ~

Neocities - The spiritual successor to GeoCities, hosting creative personal websites.
Web Design Museum - Archive of website designs from the 90s and early 2000s.
The Geocities Gallery - Preserving the best of GeoCities websites.
Internet Archaeology - Digging up and preserving digital artifacts.
One Terabyte of Kilobyte Age - Documenting GeoCities through screenshots.

~ Cool Tech Blogs ~

Retro Computing Roundup - News and articles about vintage computers.
The Dithered Web - Celebrating low-fi web aesthetics and techniques.
ASCII Art Daily - New ASCII art creations every day.
GIF Archaeology - Collecting and preserving vintage animated GIFs.
The MIDI Museum - Archive of classic MIDI files from the early web.

~ Recent Discoveries ~

Textfiles.com - Massive archive of BBS-era text files.
The Wayback Machine - Internet Archive's web page archive.
Space Jam Website - The original 1996 Space Jam website, still online!
Cameron's World - A web collage of GeoCities-era graphics.
Windows 93 - A weird and wonderful web-based OS parody.

This blogroll is updated whenever I find cool new sites. Last updated: March 28, 2025.
